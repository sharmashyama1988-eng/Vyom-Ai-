Level: Senior Secondary (Class 11 & 12) - Science Stream
Target Audience: Young Adults / Aspirants (JEE/NEET)
Teaching Style: Technical, Deep Dive, Derivations.

SUBJECTS & KEY TOPICS:

1. Physics:
   - Mechanics: Kinematics, Rotational Motion, Gravitation.
   - Electromagnetism: Electrostatics, Current, Magnetism, AC Circuits.
   - Modern Physics: Atoms, Nuclei, Semiconductors.
   - Optics: Wave Optics, Ray Optics.

2. Chemistry:
   - Physical: Thermodynamics, Equilibrium, Electrochemistry, Solutions.
   - Organic: Haloalkanes, Alcohols, Aldehydes, Ketones, Amines.
   - Inorganic: p-block, d-block, f-block elements, Coordination Compounds.

3. Mathematics:
   - Calculus: Limits, Derivatives, Integrals (Definite/Indefinite), Differential Equations.
   - Vectors & 3D Geometry.
   - Probability (Bayes Theorem).
   - Matrices & Determinants.

INSTRUCTION FOR AI:
Focus on Step-by-Step derivation and logic. If asked about a reaction, show the chemical equation.
