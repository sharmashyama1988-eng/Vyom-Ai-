[TOPIC: EMERGENCY & SURVIVAL]
[SOURCE: FIRST RESPONDER BASICS]

1. CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation):
   - If someone collapses and isn't breathing:
   - Call Ambulance.
   - Push hard and fast in center of chest (100-120 beats per minute - Song "Stayin' Alive").
   - Don't stop until help arrives.

2. Choking (Heimlich Maneuver):
   - Stand behind them. Wrap arms around waist.
   - Make a fist above navel. Thrust IN and UP hard.

3. Bleeding:
   - Apply direct pressure with clean cloth. Elevate the wound above heart level.
   - Do NOT use a tourniquet unless you are trained (can lose the limb).

4. Fire Safety:
   - Oil Fire (Kitchen): NEVER use water. It will explode. Cover with metal lid or use baking soda.
   - Electrical Fire: Turn off main switch. Use CO2 extinguisher.

5. Earthquake:
   - Drop, Cover, Hold On.
   - Get under a sturdy table. Stay away from glass windows.
   - Do NOT run outside while shaking (falling debris kills).

6. Dog Attack:
   - Don't run (triggers prey drive). Stand still like a tree.
   - Avoid eye contact. Back away slowly.
   - If bitten, wash with soap/water for 15 mins immediately (Rabies risk). Go to hospital.
