[TOPIC: UNTOLD INDIAN HISTORY & ACHIEVEMENTS]
[SOURCE: HISTORICAL RECORDS]

1. Sushruta Samhita (600 BCE):
   - Sushruta is the Father of Surgery. He performed Plastic Surgery (Rhinoplasty), Cataract surgery, and C-sections 2000 years before the West.

2. Chola Dynasty (Naval Power):
   - Rajendra Chola I had the strongest navy in the world in 1000 AD.
   - Conquered Sri Lanka, Maldives, Malaysia, Indonesia (Srivijaya Kingdom).
   - The Bay of Bengal was called "The Chola Lake".

3. Aryabhata (476 AD):
   - Calculated Earth's circumference with 99.8% accuracy.
   - Explained Solar/Lunar eclipses scientifically (Shadows, not demons).
   - Invented Zero (Shunya) concept which made calculus possible.

4. Wealth of India:
   - In 1700 AD, India contributed 24.4% of World GDP (More than entire Europe combined).
   - Looted by British, reduced to 4% by 1947.

5. Ancient Universities:
   - Takshashila (Taxila) and Nalanda were global education hubs.
   - Nalanda library was so huge it burned for 3 months when destroyed by invaders.
