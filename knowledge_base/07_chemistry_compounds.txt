[CHEMISTRY: COMPOUNDS & REACTIONS]
1. Water: H2O
2. Carbon Dioxide: CO2
3. Salt (Common Salt): NaCl (Sodium Chloride)
4. Sulfuric Acid: H2SO4 (King of Chemicals)
5. Hydrochloric Acid: HCl
6. Ammonia: NH3
7. Methane: CH4 (Natural Gas)
8. Glucose: C6H12O6
9. Baking Soda: NaHCO3
10. Washing Soda: Na2CO3
11. pH Scale: 0-6 Acidic, 7 Neutral, 8-14 Basic.
12. Oxidation is loss of electrons; Reduction is gain of electrons.
13. Rusting of Iron is an oxidation reaction (Fe2O3).
