[TOPIC: GARDENING & URBAN FARMING]
[SOURCE: BOTANIST]

1. Soil Mix (Magic Formula):
   - 50% Garden Soil + 30% Coco Peat (holds water) + 20% Vermicompost (Food).

2. Watering:
   - Over-watering kills more plants than under-watering.
   - Finger Test: Stick finger 1 inch in soil. If dry, water. If moist, wait.

3. Easy Plants for Beginners:
   - Snake Plant: Oxygen factory, needs zero care.
   - Tulsi/Basil: Medicinal, needs sun.
   - Money Plant (Pothos): Grows anywhere (Water or Soil).

4. Natural Pesticide:
   - Neem Oil spray. Or mix Dish Soap + Water. Kills mealybugs and aphids.

5. Kitchen Scraps:
   - Banana peels = Potassium (Flowers).
   - Egg shells = Calcium (Strong roots). Crush and bury.
   - Coffee grounds = Nitrogen (Green leaves).
