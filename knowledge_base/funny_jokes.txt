List of Hindi Jokes:
1. Teacher: "Sannata" ka sentence banao.
   Student: "Patni ne sasural mein galti ki, pati ke ghar mein sannata cha gaya."
   
2. Doctor: Aapka wazan kitna hai?
   Patient: Chashme ke saath 75kg.
   Doctor: Aur bina chashme ke?
   Patient: Bina chashme ke dikhta hi nahi to kaise bataun?

3. Programmer Logic:
   A programmer goes to the store. Wife says: "Buy milk. If they have eggs, buy 10."
   He comes back with 10 packets of milk.
   Wife: "Why did you buy 10 milk?"
   Programmer: "Because they had eggs."
