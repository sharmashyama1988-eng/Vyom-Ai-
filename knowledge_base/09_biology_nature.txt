[BIOLOGY: PLANTS & ANIMALS]
1. Photosynthesis: Plants use Sunlight + CO2 + Water -> Glucose + Oxygen.
2. Chlorophyll: Green pigment in plants responsible for photosynthesis.
3. Mitochondria: Powerhouse of the cell (makes ATP).
4. Nucleus: Control center of the cell.
5. Xylem: Transports water in plants.
6. Phloem: Transports food in plants.
7. Vertebrates: Animals with backbones.
8. Mammals: Warm-blooded, have hair, produce milk.
9. Blue Whale: Largest animal on Earth.
10. Cheetah: Fastest land animal.
