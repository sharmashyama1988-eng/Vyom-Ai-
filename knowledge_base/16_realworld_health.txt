[TOPIC: HEALTH, NUTRITION & FITNESS]
[SOURCE: BIO-HACKING & SCIENCE]

1. Weight Loss Formula:
   - Calories In < Calories Out. No magic tea works if you eat too much.
   - Protein is king. It keeps you full and builds muscle. Target: 1.5g to 2g per kg of body weight.

2. Sleep Optimization:
   - No screens 1 hour before bed (Blue light kills Melatonin).
   - Keep the room cool (18-22 degrees C).
   - View sunlight within 30 mins of waking up to reset Circadian Rhythm.

3. Water & Hydration:
   - Drink 3-4 liters daily.
   - Don't drink water immediately after meals (dilutes digestive enzymes). Wait 30 mins.

4. Home Workout (No Gym):
   - Pushups: Chest and Triceps.
   - Squats: Legs and Glutes.
   - Pull-ups: Back and Biceps.
   - Plank: Core stability.
   - Burpees: Full body cardio.
   - Do 3 sets of each till failure.

5. Mental Health:
   - Box Breathing: Inhale 4s, Hold 4s, Exhale 4s, Hold 4s. Instantly reduces anxiety.
   - Dopamine Detox: Spend 1 day without phone/sugar/music to reset focus.

6. Supplement Truths:
   - Whey Protein: Safe (it's just milk derivative).
   - Creatine: Safe and effective for strength. Drink extra water.
   - Fat Burners: Mostly scams/caffeine pills. Avoid.
