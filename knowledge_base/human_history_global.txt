[HISTORY & GEOPOLITICS KNOWLEDGE BASE]

1. ANCIENT CIVILIZATIONS
- Mesopotamia: The cradle of civilization (Tigris-Euphrates river system). Invented the wheel and writing (Cuneiform).
- Indus Valley: Advanced urban planning, drainage systems (Mohenjo-daro, Harappa).
- Ancient Egypt: Pyramids, Pharoahs, Hieroglyphs, Nile River agriculture.

2. WORLD WARS
- World War I (1914-1918): Allies (UK, France, Russia, US) vs Central Powers (Germany, Austria-Hungary, Ottoman). Trench warfare.
- World War II (1939-1945): Allies (US, UK, USSR) vs Axis (Germany, Japan, Italy). Holocaust, Nuclear bomb on Hiroshima/Nagasaki.

3. MODERN GEOPOLITICS
- Cold War (1947-1991): Ideological tension between USA (Capitalism) and USSR (Communism). No direct war, but proxy wars (Vietnam, Korea).
- United Nations (UN): Formed in 1945 to promote peace and cooperation.
- NATO: North Atlantic Treaty Organization. Military alliance.
- BRICS: Brazil, Russia, India, China, South Africa alliance.

4. INDIAN HISTORY (BRIEF)
- Mauryan Empire: Chandragupta Maurya, Ashoka (spread Buddhism).
- Mughal Empire: Babur, Akbar (Syncretism), Aurangzeb.
- British Raj: 1858-1947.
- Independence: 1947. Partition into India and Pakistan.
