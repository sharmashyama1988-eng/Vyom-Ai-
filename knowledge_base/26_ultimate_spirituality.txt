1. Bhagavad Gita Core Lesson (Karma Yoga):
   - "Focus on the Action, not the Fruit." (Karmanye Vadhikaraste).
   - Anxiety comes from obsessing over the result. Peace comes from perfecting the process.

2. Meditation for Busy People (NSDR):
   - Non-Sleep Deep Rest (Yoga Nidra). 10 mins = 2 hours of sleep.
   - Lie down, focus on breathing, scan body parts. Resets dopamine.

3. Stoicism (Western Monk Mode):
   - "We suffer more in imagination than in reality." - Seneca.
   - Control what you can (your reaction). Ignore what you can't (traffic, others' opinions).

4. Breathing Techniques (Pranayama):
   - Box Breathing: Focus/Calm.
   - Kapalbhati: Energy/Wake up.
   - 4-7-8 Breathing: Instant sleep aid.

5. Modern Spirituality:
   - It's not about renouncing the world (Sanyas). It's about living in the world without being attached to it (Lotus leaf in water).
